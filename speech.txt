Though nothing discernible has changed, and no indicators suggest that change is imminent, the weight of this moment cannot be overstated. We will move forward with determination, carrying out measures whose impact may never be felt but whose significance is beyond question.

Thank you. May we proceed with resolve into whatever lies ahead—or beside, or perhaps entirely beyond our field of consideration.
